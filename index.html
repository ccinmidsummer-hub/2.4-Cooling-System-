3. 冷却液流动流程图

2.4 的重点不是背零散名词，而是抓住 coolant 的动作链：pump → engine block → cylinder head → thermostat → radiator / pump；另外还有一条 separate circuit 通向 heater core。

Step 1

Pump

  • water pump sends the fluid
  • coolant starts the circulation
  • main task: move coolant through the system
Step 2

Engine

  • fluid enters the engine block
  • moves through passages around the cylinders
  • returns through the cylinder head
Step 3

Thermostat

  • closed: back to the pump directly
  • open: through the radiator first
  • purpose: control engine temperature
Step 4

Cooling / Heating

  • radiator cools the hot coolant
  • heater core forms a separate circuit
  • all paths finally return to the pump

6. 长难句精读卡

说明文精读最好做成“原句—主干—关键词—中文逻辑”四步卡片。下面挑了 2.4 中最值得精讲的三句。

句 1｜系统功能总句

The cooling system helps regulate the amount of heat in the engine.

句子主干

The cooling system helps regulate the amount of heat in the engine.
冷却系统帮助调节发动机中的热量。这是全文的功能总句，后面所有部件都在服务这个目标。

关键词提示

  • helps regulate 表示“帮助调节”。
  • the amount of heat 是固定表达，表示“热量的多少”。
  • engine 是整个系统服务的中心对象。

句 2｜节温器控制句

It blocks coolant flow to the radiator until the engine reaches a predetermined temperature, which allows the engine to warm up quickly.

句子主干

It blocks coolant flow to the radiator until the engine reaches a predetermined temperature.
在发动机达到预设温度之前，节温器会阻止冷却液流向散热器，这样发动机才能更快升温。

关键词提示

  • until 引导时间逻辑。
  • predetermined temperature 表示“预设温度”。
  • which allows... 是结果补充。

句 3｜工作过程核心句

The plumbing around the thermostat sends the fluid back to the pump directly if the thermostat is closed. If it is open, the fluid goes through the radiator first and then back to the pump.

句子主干

closed → back to the pump directly; open → through the radiator first.
这两句构成 2.4 最核心的“分支流程图”，也是理解冷却系统工作逻辑的关键。

关键词提示

  • if the thermostat is closed / open 是条件对比。
  • back to the pump directly 对应“小循环”。
  • through the radiator first 对应“经过散热器的大循环”。

2.4 Cooling System — Introduction
The cooling system helps regulate the amount of heat in the engine.
If the engine is too hot, it can overheat.
If it's too cold, the engine emits more pollutants and engine components prematurely wear out.
If the cooling system fails to keep the engine at the right temperature, it can suffer significant damage and, in some cases, fail entirely.
冷却系统有助于调节发动机中的热量。如果发动机过热，就会发生过热故障；如果温度过低，发动机会排放更多污染物，而且零部件会过早磨损。如果冷却系统不能让发动机保持在合适温度，发动机就可能遭受严重损坏，某些情况下甚至会完全失效。
Key Parts of the Cooling System重点理解 water pump、radiator、thermostat、expansion tank、radiator cap、fan assembly 和 coolant 的功能。
这一节适合课堂边讲边指图：先问“what does it do”，再问“why is it necessary”。
03
Water Pump
The water pump, also known as the coolant pump, circulates coolant through the engine's cooling system.
水泵，也叫冷却液泵，负责让冷却液在发动机冷却系统中循环。
04
Radiator
The radiator receives hot coolant from the engine.
As the coolant passes through the radiator's collection of small tubes, it is cooled before returning to the engine.
散热器接收来自发动机的高温冷却液。冷却液流经散热器内部许多细小管道时会被冷却，然后再返回发动机。
05
Thermostat
The thermostat is a mechanical device that controls engine temperature.
It blocks coolant flow to the radiator until the engine reaches a predetermined temperature, which allows the engine to warm up quickly.
节温器是一种控制发动机温度的机械装置。在发动机达到预定温度之前，它会阻止冷却液流向散热器，这样有助于发动机快速暖机。
06
Coolant Expansion Tank
As engine coolant heats up, it expands.
The coolant expansion tank provides additional storage for the expanding coolant and is commonly the location where coolant is added to the engine.
发动机冷却液受热后会膨胀。冷却液膨胀箱为膨胀后的冷却液提供额外储存空间，而且通常也是向发动机添加冷却液的位置。
07
Radiator Cap
The radiator cap seals the main filling point of the cooling system.
It also allows a set pressure to build in the system, which increases the boiling point—and thus, the efficiency—of the cooling system.
散热器盖密封冷却系统的主加注口。它还允许系统内部建立一定压力，从而提高冷却液的沸点，也就提高了冷却系统的效率。
08
Radiator Fan Assembly
The radiator fan assembly, which helps to cool the hot coolant in the radiator, is comprised of cooling fan(s), cooling fan motor(s), and a fan shroud.
散热器风扇总成帮助冷却散热器中的高温冷却液，它由冷却风扇、风扇电机和风扇护罩组成。
09
Coolant
Coolant is the fluid that flows through the engine's cooling system.
Its main purpose is to transfer heat generated by the engine to the radiator so the engine doesn't overheat.
冷却液是在发动机冷却系统中流动的液体，它的主要作用是把发动机产生的热量传递到散热器，从而防止发动机过热。
How Cooling System Works抓住 coolant 的流动路线：pump → engine → thermostat → radiator / heater core → pump。
读这部分时建议学生边看流程图边朗读，尤其要分清 thermostat closed 和 thermostat open 两种情况。
10
How Cooling System Works
The pump sends the fluid into the engine block, where it makes its way through passages in the engine around the cylinders.
Then it returns through the cylinder head of the engine.
The thermostat is located where the fluid leaves the engine.
The plumbing around the thermostat sends the fluid back to the pump directly if the thermostat is closed.
If it is open, the fluid goes through the radiator first and then back to the pump.
水泵先把冷却液送入发动机缸体，冷却液随后通过发动机内部围绕气缸的通道流动，然后经由气缸盖返回。节温器位于冷却液离开发动机的位置。如果节温器关闭，周围的管路会把冷却液直接送回水泵；如果节温器打开，冷却液会先流经散热器，然后再回到水泵。
11
Heating Circuit
There is also a separate circuit for the heating system.
This circuit takes fluid from the cylinder head and passes it through a heater core and then back to the pump.
此外，供暖系统还有一个独立回路。这个回路从气缸盖引出冷却液，让它流经暖风芯体，然后再回到水泵。
